Paper prototyping

For this project we were split into groups of 4 to 5 people.
We made a concept for a game not knowing what was next
after concepting a tiny game we were told we had to make "paper prototypes",
naturally we thought we had to do some work with cutting paper and stuff.
after we cut up the cards to match our player character we were told the playing cards were ment for...
playing the cards not cutting them up into pieces to look like something else.
This was a pretty funny twist but we continued with normal paper anyway otherwise we wouldn't have had a finished product.
We ended up making a 2d arena where you had to dodge incoming snakes (operated by another player) to survive.
So we finished the paper prototype and presented it expecting some bad feedback,
due to it being lackluster compared to other projects which were full fledged card and board games.
But no! We actually got pretty good feedback like; "Creative twist" and "I like the idea, reminds me of cuphead".
So it all turned out okay and we were quite happy with the end result.
